Child Safety Standards Policy

App Name: MyLeaders

Developer: MyLeaders Team

1. Commitment to Child Safety

MyLeaders is committed to ensuring the safety and protection of children. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that involves, promotes, or facilitates CSAE.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Uploading, sharing, or distributing content involving sexual abuse of minors
  • Any sexually explicit content involving children
  • Grooming, exploitation, or inappropriate interaction with minors
  • Attempting to solicit or engage minors in harmful or illegal activities

3. Reporting and Enforcement

We provide mechanisms for users to report inappropriate or harmful behavior. Reports can be submitted via:

  • In-app reporting features (if available)
  • Email: myleaders.support@ezemp.com

Upon receiving a report, we will take immediate action, including:

  • Content removal
  • Account suspension or termination
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities where required

4. Compliance with Laws

MyLeaders complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations in cases involving CSAE.

5. Content Moderation

We implement proactive and reactive measures to detect and prevent harmful content, including:

  • User reporting systems
  • Content review and moderation
  • Removal of violating content

6. Contact Information

If you have any concerns regarding child safety, please contact us at:

Email: myleaders.support@ezemp.com
